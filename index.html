About Us

We are a group of high school students working on a project for our government class. For legal reasons, this website is purely for a school project and should not be taken seriously in any way.
What we Do

We're here to spread some facts about what is happening in Russia under Putin's control. - Putin led Russia into a war against Ukraine, claiming it to be to liberate Ukraine from Nazism, but there is no evidence of such in Ukraine - Putin has taken control of the media, and any large news source is controled and censored by the government - Prominent independent outlets are declared "foreign agents": Meduza, VTimes, Dozhd, OVD-Info, Mediazona, and iStories - Putin portrays the war as relatively peaceful, claiming that around 6,000 Russian soldiers have died. Western estimates puts that number around 100,000. - Putin blames Russian war crimes on the West. After pulling out of Bucha, it was revealed there were over 50 civilian deaths to Russian troops, which Putin blamed on Ukraine. Over 1,200 civilian bodies were found in Kyiv after Russian forces withdrew - Putin has forced the deportation of thousands of civilians to Russia and there are reports of rape and sexual assult of Ukrainian civilians by Russian forces - Putin is silencing any opposition to the war, with vague laws which can be used to imprison or fine any protestors - Harsh police force is used against protestors, including detaining over 11,000 civilians in protests after Aleksey Navalny's imprisonment - Aleksey Navalny, the only true competitor to Putin in 2018 was disqualified for alleged politically motivated crimes. In 2020, he was poisoned on a flight to Moscow - Putin rigs the elections in other ways. By changing the way Duma seats are elected, his party recieved 72% of the seats with less than 50% of the vote in 2021 - Putin has banned extremist organizations from elections and uses a vote threshold to secure even more seats while destroying smaller parties - Putin controls the government from the top down, such that no person in power is independent And so much more...
